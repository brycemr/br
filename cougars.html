This confidence can also translate into better communication and a stronger sense of self-worth. No Games: Older women have typically outgrown the games and drama that can come with dating younger women. They know what they want and aren't afraid to ask for it. Overall, dating older women can bring many benefits, from intellectual conversations to emotional stability, confidence, and great sex.
